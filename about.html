About Us

ZUNO started in 2021 when the CEO, Reyna Chelsie Zuno saw businesses struggle through the pandemic and wanted to find ways to help them succeed. She wanted to help businesses in her neighborhood by helping them market their resources and products correctly through their social media platforms, whether it was through photography or marketing strategies. ZUNO continues to do this with small companies, brands and organizations to further help them achieve their goals within their businesses.
